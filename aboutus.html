Why AutoUnit?

As the average age increases across the western world it is becoming more and more important to design our environment with people with reduced mobility in mind. Back pain is a common problem, especially amongst the elderly. Previous studies conducted indicate that 36-70% of older adults (65+) experience back pain, with an even wider 65-85% reporting muscoskeletal pain.

Our motivation for this project comes deep from our hearts, all of us have seen our grandparents struggle as they get older. We want to help people overcome their mobility issues and provide real world solutions to their most common problems. This starts with the AutoUnit.
