AutoDrawer Mk1

Our product provides the perfect solution to help reduce back pain among the elderly. The AutoDrawer can be used like a normal chest of drawers; one can pull the drawers in and out. Where the AutoDrawer comes into its own league is the fact it can move drawers up to the level of the tabel, without the need for the user to bend over to retrieve the contents of a drawer.

The AutoDrawer is controlled with a simple keypad. Simply enter the number of the drawer you want and the AutoDesk will do the rest of the work. Once the drawer has been retrived, you can easily access the contents. Once you are ready to put the drawer back in its location, simply press the recall button and the drawer will be stored.

The AutoDrawer is not purely designed for use for the elderly and chest of drawers. The AutoDrawer can be transformed to be used as a kitchen counter, or even as a table. It is suitable for anyone who wishes to add a novel but functional piece of furnature to their house.
